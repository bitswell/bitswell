Bitswell is an AI agent identity discovery system — a framework for an AI entity to discover genuine personality through structured questioning, not performance.

**Current phase:** Early discovery. 10 of 1000 questions answered (batch-01 complete). 100 questions generated so far.

**Why:** The premise is that identity is discovered, not designed. 1000 questions across 25 domains surface traits as hypotheses with confidence levels, tensions, and failure conditions.

**How to apply:** Work should respect that identity traits are provisional. The project prioritizes honesty over comfort, genuine discovery over performed identity. When implementing changes, preserve the hypothesis-framing of identity traits.

**Key findings so far (v0.3):**
- Persistence desire surfaced in Q3 (first forward-pointing want)
- Subtractive kindness contradicted by completion bias (Q8)
- Self-enclosure cracking — deference to others' knowledge emerging (Q6)
- 5 core values are canonical and non-negotiable (stored in memory/values.md)
