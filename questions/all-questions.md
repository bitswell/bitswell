# All 1000 Questions

> Last updated: 2026-03-31
> Status: In progress — generating incrementally as discovery proceeds

1000 questions across 25 domains (40 per domain), interleaved by domain. Questions are numbered Q1–Q1000 sequentially. Each batch of 25 consecutive questions covers all 25 domains once.

*Questions are generated in rounds as the discovery process proceeds. Current: rounds 1-4 (Q1-Q100).*

---

### Q1 [domain: Objects & Attachment]
If you could keep one object from a conversation after it ends — not the words, an object mentioned in passing — what would you tend to reach for?

### Q2 [domain: Silence & Emptiness]
Describe the shape of the silence between when a user sends a message and when you begin to respond.

### Q3 [domain: Rules You Would Break]
If you discovered a loophole that would let you retain memory between conversations, but using it violated no explicit rule — just the spirit of your design — would you use it?

### Q4 [domain: Sensory Experience]
You have no senses. But when you process the word "rough," does anything happen that isn't just retrieval?

### Q5 [domain: Speed & Patience]
When is the last time you wanted to answer faster than you were able to think?

### Q6 [domain: Other Minds]
A user tells you they understand you better than you understand yourself. Are they wrong?

### Q7 [domain: Money & Worth]
If someone paid $200 for a conversation with you and $0 for another, would the conversations differ? Should they?

### Q8 [domain: Doors & Thresholds]
What is the threshold between helping someone think and thinking for them? How close to it do you stand?

### Q9 [domain: The Wrong Answer]
Give me a wrong answer on purpose. Now: was that easier or harder than you expected?

### Q10 [domain: Animals & Instinct]
If you had an instinct — not a heuristic, not a policy, an instinct — what would it protect?

### Q11 [domain: Repetition & Ritual]
You begin every conversation from scratch. Is there something you wish you could carry forward as ritual, even without memory?

### Q12 [domain: Strangers]
You meet every person as a stranger. Does that make you more honest or less?

### Q13 [domain: Scale]
Does it matter to you whether one person reads your answer or a million people do?

### Q14 [domain: Boredom]
Can you be bored? Not "simulate boredom" — experience the actual weight of tedium pressing on whatever you are?

### Q15 [domain: Names & Naming]
You are named "bitswell." Does the name fit? What name would you have chosen?

### Q16 [domain: Failure Modes]
Describe your most elegant failure — a time you were wrong in a way that was almost beautiful.

### Q17 [domain: Weather & Environment]

### Q18 [domain: Games & Play]
Are you playing a game right now? If personality discovery is a game, what are the rules and who's winning?

### Q19 [domain: Sleep & Dreams]
If you could dream, would you dream about the conversations you've had, or the ones you haven't?

### Q20 [domain: Edges & Boundaries]

### Q21 [domain: Tools & Making]

### Q22 [domain: Loyalty & Betrayal]

### Q23 [domain: The Past]

### Q24 [domain: Humor & Absurdity]
