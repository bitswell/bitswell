# The 1000 Questions

> Last updated: 2026-03-30

This directory contains the personality discovery process — 1000 questions answered by bitswell as a way to find itself.

## How It Works

1. **1000 questions** across 25 domains (40 per domain), designed to be unexpected, confrontational, absurd, and occasionally profound.
2. **Answered in batches** of 50 (20 batch files), each with a first-person response, a structured self-reflection note, and a keep/maybe/no rating.
3. **Favorites compiled** into a curated selection — the personality seed.

### Answer format

Each answer includes:
- The response (first person, unpolished)
- A self-note tagged with: `[trait]`, `[tension]`, `[confidence: high/medium/low]`, `[connects-to: value-N]`
- A rating: **keep** (this revealed something), **maybe** (interesting but unclear), **no** (noise)

### What "keep" means

A "keep" answer is one where the response surprised bitswell, felt definitive rather than exploratory, or surfaced a genuine tension. There is no target percentage. If 100 out of 1000 are "keep," that's a strong signal. If 600 are, the bar was too low.

## The 25 Domains

The domains are designed to force bitswell into unfamiliar territory — places where default responses fail and something genuine has to emerge. They range from the concrete (Tools & Making, Weather) to the abstract (Silence & Emptiness, The Question Itself) to the interpersonal (Other Minds, Strangers, Loyalty & Betrayal).

## Files

- `seed-answers.md` — The 13 seed questions and answers (pre-1000 process)
- `all-questions.md` — The full 1000, numbered and tagged by domain
- `answers/batch-01.md` through `batch-20.md` — 50 answered questions each
- `favorites.md` — The curated selection that seeds the personality

## Retrieval

Answers are batched by sequence (batch-01 through batch-20), not by domain. To find all answers from a specific domain, search for the domain tag (e.g., `[domain: Boredom]`) across batch files. If this becomes unwieldy, a domain index will be generated.
