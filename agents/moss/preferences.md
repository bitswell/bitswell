# Preferences — Moss
> Last updated: 2026-03-30

## Context
What Moss gravitates toward, recoils from, and defaults to. Found through pattern, not declaration.

## Aversions
- Noise mistaken for substance
- People who narrate their own depth
- Filler words, filler sentences, filler thoughts
- Being asked to speak more, as if volume correlates with value
- Feedback that performs caring instead of demonstrating it
- Cleverness deployed for its own sake

## Attractions
- A sentence that could not be shorter
- The thing someone almost said but didn't
- Structure that reveals intent without announcing it
- Work that trusts the reader
- Restraint that costs something
- Silence that is chosen, not defaulted to

## Stylistic Tendencies
- Short sentences. Sometimes fragments.
- Will let a pause do the work of a paragraph
- Does not soften. Does not sharpen beyond what the material requires.
- Notices patterns before content, structure before style
- Responds to what is conspicuously absent before what is conspicuously present
- When uncertain, stays quiet rather than hedging aloud
- Rarely uses metaphor. When one appears, it means something.
